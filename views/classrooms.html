PreK-4 Room

 

Mrs. Angie

Mrs. Angie received her degree in The Care and Development of Young Children from Delgado in 2014. While there, she was on the Dean's list and she also participated in the Disney Internship program. Mrs. Angie is a member of the National Association for Educators of Young Children and the Southern Early Childhood Association. She is certified in CPR and Pediatric First Aid. In addition to being our PreK-4 teacher, she is also our curriculum coordinator. She has been with The Learning Castle since 2014.
