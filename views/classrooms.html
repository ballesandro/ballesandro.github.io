Mrs. Angie received her degree in The Care and Development of Young Children in 2014. While there, she was on the Dean's list and she also participated in the Disney Internship program. Mrs. Angie is a member of the National Association for Educators of Young Children. She is certified in CPR and Pediatric First Aid.
