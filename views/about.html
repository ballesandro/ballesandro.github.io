Meet our royal staff!

 

Ms. Stacey

Stacey studied at U.N.O. for three years before receiving her diploma in Child Daycare Management in 1999 from Harcourt University. She has been the proud owner of four successful daycare centers in Jefferson Parish, starting in 2000. Since that time, she has achieved classification as a Level Three Director under the Louisiana Quality Start program. She is also a Continuing Education Trainer with the LA Pathways program of Northwestern State University, Louisiana. Stacey is a Designated Toddler Class Observer with the Louisiana Department of Education and is Certified in CPR, Pediatric First Aid, and Medication Administration. She is a childcare advocate who has testified in Baton Rouge before the BESE board on behalf of childcare centers statewide, and she has been a member of the Southern Early Childhood association for several years.
